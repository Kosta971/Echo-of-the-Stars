Echo of the Stars

When the stars began to whisper, only a few could hear...

Story

In the distant future, humanity has spread across the stars. But a strange phenomenon begins: voices coming from the stars, driving people mad. Only a few can hear them without losing their minds. Among them is Ira Nomiya, a young pilot with a forgotten past. Her journey will uncover the true nature of the voices—and herself.
